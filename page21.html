Monthly Water Testing

Quick Reminders

-3T water sampling is done monthly (within 30 days).

-Phigenics will supply shipping box, sample container, and return shipping label.

-Each heater cooler (HC) will need 4 samples: 2 pre/2 post.

-Sampling for: Heterotrophic Plate Count (HPC) and Myobacterium (NTM).

-HPC 150ml container (fill to top of container).

-NTM 250ml container (fill to top of container).

-Refer to Liva Nova 3T IFU section 6.5.2 for reference/ instructions for sampling.
Click Here

-Samples need to be taken and shipped out same day
-HPC samples need to be refrigerated (minimum of 2 ice packs)
-HPC samples are sent to Nevada and NTM samples are sent to Arkansas
    

1. Spray the drain with the Cavi spray and let set for 5 minutes. 

2. Drain 500ml before taking a pre or post sample.

3. Place correct sample in correct box with included chain of custody paper.

4. Place sealed box on shipping cart on the first floor before 1500 Monday-Thursday.
