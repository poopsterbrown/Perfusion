LivaNova 3T Heater/Coolers

Standard Cleaning Quick Guide

1. Drain Heater/Cooler. Close drainage release values when done.

2. Wipe down machine with Caviwipes.

3. Remove tubes and water cap from heater/cooler. Spray each port with caviwipe spray 4 times and let set for 5 minutes. 

4. Wipe off access caviwipe spray for 2 minutes. 

5. Reconnect hoses in appropriate manor for mixing. 

6. Ensure the drain nozzles are closed and begin to fill the heater/cool with distilled water. Stop filling once the orange light (click here for picture) blinks the second reservoir indicator. Add in 150ml of 3% hydrogen peroxide. Continue filling with water until the light indicator shows that the heater/cooler is full (click here for picture). Do not continue to fill after all the lights are green. Finally place the cap back on the machine.

7. Adjust the settings:
Cold: 10
Warm: 20
Press the cold cardioplegia button and let run for 5 minutes. 
Click here for a picture. 

8. Set tubing back to normal. Spray/wipe as needed.

9. Replace suction canister and tubing.

10. Sample water to ensure hydrogen peroxide concentration.
